BYU-Idaho Github Course Pages

Welcome to the BYU-I Math Department Github course page. You will find links to courses that have github.io pages below. All courses are maintained through the byuistats github group. For now, these are generally the classes that J. Hathaway teaches.

Statistics & Data Science Courses

Active

CSE 150 - Data Intuition and Insight

We also host the supplemental readings for CSE 150 which are largely taken from the Math 221 and Math 325 textbooks.

CSE 250 - Data Science Programming

We host the Python for Data Science book as well.

M325 - Intermediate Statistics

This is the course textbook. Updates are made more often at Garrett Saunders repository.

M335 - Data Wrangling and Visualization

This course started in the fall of 2017. Other teachers may host their material in our learning management system.

M488 - Statistical and Data Science Consulting

We are in draft mode for this page. The course has been around for a few years. We are modernizing it to handle the inlfux of data science students.

M498 - Data Science Internship

This pages contains the syllabus and some information to help with searching for an internship. You can also find internship information at byuidatascience.github.io

M499 - Data Science Senior Project

Each data science major will complete a 3-credit senior project with one of the data science faculty.

M221(ABC) - Introduction to Statistics

Our course textbook is now hosted using bookdown. This course is largely maintained in BYU-I’s learning management system. We have a few material items here for the faculty to use. J. Hathaway stores some of his course items on his own M221 page
